# Warranty & Support

## Warranty Coverage

Devices purchased directly from Wee Noise Makers have a warranty of **six months** from the date of purchase. This warranty may be extended depending on local regulations.

### What's Covered

This warranty covers manufacturing defects in the device, including:

- Hardware component failures
- Assembly defects
- Electronic malfunctions under normal use

### What's Not Covered

The warranty does not cover damage due to:

- Incorrect handling or storage
- Improper power supply or overvoltage events
- Physical damage (drops, impacts, liquid exposure)
- Modifications to hardware or software
- Normal wear and tear
- Use outside normal operating conditions

## Warranty Claims

### Process

1. Contact Wee Noise Makers via the website contact form
2. Describe the issue in detail
3. Include your order information
4. Follow support team instructions

### Resolution

Devices returned under warranty will be:

- Refunded, OR
- Replaced, OR
- Repaired

at Wee Noise Makers' discretion.

### Important Notes

- User data may not be preserved during repair/replacement
- Customer is responsible for return shipping costs
- Keep your proof of purchase

## Modding and Hacking

The PGB-1 is open source and designed to be extended and modified by users.

### What This Means

- Hardware schematics and firmware source code are available
- Users can modify the device
- Custom firmware can be installed

### Risks

Hardware modification or installation of third-party firmware can lead to:

- Irreversible damage to the device
- Loss of functionality
- Safety issues

### Warranty Impact

!!! warning "Important"
    Any modification will **void the warranty**.

This includes:

- Hardware modifications (soldering, component changes)
- Installation of unofficial firmware
- Opening the device case
- Connecting non-standard peripherals

### Wee Noise Makers' Position

Wee Noise Makers:

- Will not be accountable for damage from modifications
- Will not refund, repair, or replace modified products
- Cannot provide support for modified devices

Modifying the device could also void the user's authority to operate the equipment under applicable regulations.
